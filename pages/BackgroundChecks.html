Background Checks

For a name change, you will need to get a state and a federal background check, and you will need to get fingerprinted for each. Once you get these results back, you have 90 days to file your application for a name change with the court.

Note: Since you have not actually changed your name yet, you need to use your old name for these forms.


National Identity History Summary

This is your federal background check. It is fastest to complete it online, but you can do it by mail. After submitting your request, you will get an FBI order confirmation number and instructions for how to submit your fingerprints for this background check. After sending your fingerprints, you will receive the results of your background check by either email or physical mail.

Fingerprinting - Federal

Fingerprinting for federal background checks requires a USPS post office that offers fingerprinting service (which can be found at the above link). You will need to do this after requesting your National Identity History Summary, as you will need your order FBI order confirmation number to register to get fingerprinted at the post office.

Note: You can skip this step by getting a second fingerprint card while getting your fingerprints for your state background check. However, you will have to mail that second fingerprint card, which will cause the federal background check to take much longer.

Fingerprinting - State

You will need to find this on your own (sorry) as it varies by county (if you happen to live in Wake county, you can get fingerprinted here). You should get a fingerprint card from here that you will need to send with your Right to Review request.

Right to Review

This is your request for a state background check (found here). After you get your National Identity History Summary results back, you will need to print and mail the final page of this form with your fingerprint card and either a certified check or money order after you get your National Identity History Summary results back. You will receive your results back by mail.
