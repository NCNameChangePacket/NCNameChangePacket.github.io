Welcome

Hello. Welcome to my NC Name Change Packet. This was something I wish I had going through this process. Hopefully it will save you some of the struggling I needed to go through to find all these forms.

For context, I went through this process while legally transitioning as an adult (the process is different for people under 18) and living in Wake County, NC in early 2024. I am not a lawyer, and so I cannot give legal advice (although here are the general statutes on name changes, G.S. 101, if you are interested). That said, I am somebody who had to stumble through this complicated process. My hope is that this guide will help make this process (at least slightly) less complicated for you.
